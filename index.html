About Us

BE BEAUTIFUL, FEEL BEAUTIFUL, LOOK BEAUTIFUL INSIDE AND OUT


The company ‘GirlZ destination’ deals with the variety of make-up products. Founded in 2000, at model town Ludhiana India, known for its unique, open-sell environment, a variety of brands and their vast range of different products and its own GIRLZ COLLECTION. Today, GirlZ destination is not the only leading retailer of beauty products in India, but also in some other Asian countries. We started by looking at mother nature. We formulate the products that honor that inspiration while delivering cutting-edge cosmetics. Our goal is to provide healthy, clean and pure cosmetics. The company does business mainly in the line of feminine related beauty products. It has the long range of products in different categories like makeup, skin care, hair care and tools & brushes. The specialty of girl’s destination is that it offers everyday deals sometimes discounted and even sometimes are of free offering. The company has a-z brands in which featured are Huda Beauty, Marc Jacobs, Loreal, Avon and many more. The company sells its products from stores. In every store, customers can get products of every brand. People can go to stores and are attended by the experienced and well-patient employees who also help them to make purchases. Moreover, the company also provide training classes to learners about women health and beauty to make a career in the field of the beauty world. Along with this they also provide training classes to their employees so that they have good knowledge about the different taste of the customers. We also organize activities from time to time for women empowerment. Girlz destination believes in the role of a code of conduct from their employees which prohibits them from human trafficking, harassment, child labor and abuse. Our code of conduct also addresses working conditions for suppliers and specifically restrict the use of involuntary activities. Girlz destination also provide training to ensure its employees with direct responsibility for product supply chain management are knowledgeable and aware of the issues and concerns of supply products.
